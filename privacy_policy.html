Privacy Policy

Your data, your trust — protected.

Pinnakey Connect collects and processes information only to verify authorship, improve transparency, and strengthen the craft ecosystem.

Information we collect

● Identification documents, contact details, and demographic data
● Craft and process-related material such as photos, videos, and descriptions
● Technical data such as device type, IP address, and usage analytics

Use of information

● To verify and publish artisan or craft profiles
● To improve the accuracy and accessibility of the Platform
● To maintain a public record of verified craft data

Data protection

All information is stored securely using encryption and limited access protocols. Only authorised personnel are allowed to process data for legitimate purposes.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by writing to our team. Once verified, your information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for commercial use.
We value your trust and are committed to maintaining confidentiality and transparency in every aspect of our work.
