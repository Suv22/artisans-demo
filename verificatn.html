Verification Standards

Authenticity is at the heart of what we do.

Every verified artisan, craft, and cluster on Pinnakey Connect undergoes a rigorous process designed to safeguard authorship, ensure transparency, and build lasting trust. Our verification model combines human expertise with digital intelligence to confirm that every identity, material, and process can be traced and trusted.

How verification works

1. Identity Verification: Craftspersons are validated through documentary proof, peer recognition, and field-level checks.
2. Craft Provenance: Each practice is matched with its historical and geographical origins using official records, lineage data, and material evidence.
3. Process Validation: Step-by-step documentation confirms that the technique aligns with its traditional method or an accepted evolution of it.
4. Digital Authorship Passport: Once verified, a unique digital profile is created that links the artisan’s story, process, and product metadata.

Use of information

● To verify and publish artisan or craft profiles
● To improve the accuracy and accessibility of the Platform
● To maintain a public record of verified craft data
Our verification standards are guided by integrity, inclusivity, and transparency. Recognition is not a privilege. It is a right.

Data protection

All information is stored securely using encryption and limited access protocols. Only authorised personnel are allowed to process data for legitimate purposes.

Who can Get Verified


Your craft. Your name. Your proof of authorship.

Pinnakey Connect helps artisans, collectives, and organisations receive verified digital authorship, giving every creator a voice and identity that travels with their work.

Who can apply

● Individual artisans and craft clusters
● NGOs, cooperatives, and foundations
● Designers, researchers, and institutions working with traditional crafts

What you receive

● Verified Digital Authorship Passport
● Personalised online profile and QR-linked identity card
● Access to collaborations and national–international visibility

How it works

1. Fill out the verification form.
2. Submit basic identification and documentation of your craft process.
3. Our team reviews the submission for authenticity and alignment with standards.
4. Upon approval, you will receive your verified authorship profile.

By submitting your details, you confirm that the information provided is true and that you have the right to represent the work submitted for verification.
